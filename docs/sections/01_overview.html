Overview

Time-series signals appear across domains such as seismic monitoring, physiological sensing, and industrial systems, and many applications need event retrieval, not just classification or forecasting. In this project we convert fixed time segments into vector embeddings, index them for similarity search, and compare retrieval quality across curated datasets from EarthScope/IRIS, PhysioNet, and the UCR Time Series Classification Archive. We evaluate how signal representations, raw time windows, frequency features from FFT or STFT, and mixed time-frequency views, interact with different embedding approaches across domains, aiming to identify combinations that yield strong, transferable retrieval behavior.
